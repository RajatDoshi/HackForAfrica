Connecting Doctors Who Serve To Patients In Need.

Disease Does Not Know Boundaries.

So Why Should Medicine?



Africa has 25% of the world's diseased and 2% of the world's doctors.

17% of the world has less than 1% of the global health expenditure.

Let's get to work.

The facts:


Doctors

Doctors: we need your help in combating the ongoing crisis in Africa. You've seen the statistics. Sick patients around the globe are unable to get the coverage they need, especially during a global pandemic. Through the help of doctors like you from around the world, we can come to the aid of those suffering in this continent-wide doctor shortage.
Our Triage System

We ensure that the patients who need the most urgent care are matched up with doctors who can best help them through our state-of-the-art triage system on this all-in-one platform. By incorporating both a severity classification, through our preliminary AI diagnoser, and a specialist matching system, we make this possible.
Join the Discussion

It's no secret that COVID-19 has impacted the world as a whole, impacting the way we approach medicine. No matter where you are reading this from, chances are you either know of a patient with limited healthcare access or a licensed physician who can provide the services that so many desperately need. Help us spread our message and improve our platform.
How We Help & Mission: A telemedicine approach

In the modern age of telemedicine, it's vital that the services provided by so many doctors around the world can be afforded by those who require them. We provide a self-sufficient platform for this entire process. This includes evaluating the patient's clincal status, scheduling them with an appropriate specialist, and providing a video and message interface.
